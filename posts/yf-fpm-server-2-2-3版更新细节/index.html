[YF-FPM-SERVER] 2.2.3版更新细节

Wed, Apr 19, 2017

本次更新主要是移除了一些内核中可插件化的代码，并新增了2个插件 fpm-plugin-qiniu-upload,fpm-plugin-socketio 。2.2.3版本将是一个lts版本。

依赖变更

  • remove qiniu

最新的依赖

  "koa": "^2.0.0",
  "koa-bodyparser": "^3.2.0",
  "koa-multer": "^1.0.1",
  "koa-router": "^7.1.0",
  "koa2-cors": "^2.0.3",
  "lodash": "^4.16.1",
  "md5": "^2.1.0",
  "moment": "^2.13.0",
  "pubsub-js": "^1.5.5"

移除的特性

  • remove upload 的路由
this.app.use(upload.routes()).use(upload.allowedMethods())

    • github: https://github.com/team4yf/fpm-plugin-qiniu-upload

  • 集成socketio服务插件 fpm-plugin-socketio

    • github: https://github.com/team4yf/fpm-plugin-socketio
